Penne Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound penne pasta
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook the penne pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water according to package instructions, until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and black pepper. Stir until the cheese has melted and the sauce is smooth.
  4. Add the cooked penne pasta to the saucepan and stir until the pasta is coated with the sauce.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Penne Alfredo!
